Hi, I'm Cristina

A Colorful Journey

Being an Aries with Pisces moon has created a colorful, yet challenging life. Balancing bold impulses with a desire to constantly get lost in fantasty is tricky business.

Yes, I am a laugher...

But I am also extremely shy. What am I doing on many a Friday night? Reading at a bar. You know, so I can be around people without having to talk to people.

I'm also a huge goof...

You won't often find me being embarrassed to break out the running man in Barnes & Noble or make a silly face waiting for ice cream.
